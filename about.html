Our Background

Great Harvest Bank SACCO (GHBS) was established in 2016 and got registered on the 26th of October 2021

Objectives

  • A viable SACCO ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 1)
  • Robust Institutional capacity built with structures and capabilities ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 2)
  • A technology enabled SACCO ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 3)
  • Value Generated partnerships ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 4)

Strategy

Vision

A countrywide prosperous member-focused Savings, Credit and investment institution by 2028

Mission

To grow savings and improve members’ welfare through providing affordable products and services while ensuring institutional profitability and sustainability

Core Values

Integrity, Honesty, Transparency, Equity & Fairness, Excellent Customer Care

A viable SACCO ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 1)
  1. Grow a motivated membership base through offering attractive products and services
  2. Invest in risk managed opportunities for high and medium returns
  3. Leverage SACCO assets with debt and undertake profitable joint ventures
  4. Build and support members' capacity to develop viable micro-enterprises
  5. Broaden membership eligibility criteria to include extended family members
Robust Institutional capacity built with structures and capabilities ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 2)
  1. Build and strengthen SACCO governance
  2. Competent management skills
  3. Institute and operate efficient internal processes
A technology enabled SACCO ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 3)
  1. Invest in digital systems to automate business processes
  2. Preserve SACCO and customer assets with effective cyber security technologies
  3. Promote appropriate technologies in member business
Value Generated partnerships ( Strategic Ogjactives under Goal 4)
  1. Enter into strategic alliances with prospective organizations
  2. Explore and exploit available institutional development and member focused grant oportunities
  3. Develop a market for onward lending opportunities amongst members
